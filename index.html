Hi, I am Jiacheng Xu, a research scientist at Salesforce AI Research.

I passed my Ph.D. defense from the Department of Computer Science at the UT Austin. I worked in the Text Analysis, Understanding, and Reasoning Group under Greg Durrett. My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, specifically the area of text generation.

        (1) Long chain-of-actions for agentic code llms (Xu'25, Zhang'25, ongoing;) ;
        (2) Decoding algorithms to yield a massive number of high-quality and diverse outputs (XJD22, XXSZ23, SXYD23; slides) ;

Before I came to UT Austin, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Fudan University in 2017. From 2015 to 2017, I was a member of FudanNLP, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu.
